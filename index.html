Where To Go On Your Trip

Tobago

Outside the hurricane belt of the West Indes and 7 miles from Venezuela, Tobago should be on everyone's vacation bucket list. White Sandy beaches, pleanty of glass bottom excursions and an abundance of local shops and restuarants including the famous Shark N Bake will be an experience long remembered.

Fly to Trinidad and connect with Ferry early in morning.

********************************************************************

Curacao

Secluded, warm and inviting, Curacao contains the warmth of the Caribbean and the exotic tendencies of South America for an unforgettable vacation experience.

Fly into Venezuela and take private boat to resort.

********************************************************************

Aruba

Aruba is a Dutch island getaway. It has old world charm with warm tropical beaches and extremely friendly people. A getaway to get away and soak the sun while enjoying a vibrant nightlife.

Fly into Jamiaca and transfer to Caribbean Air.
